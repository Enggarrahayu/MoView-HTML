MOANA 2

"Moana 2" takes audiences on a new journey with Moana, Maui, and a new crew made up of unique sailors. After receiving an unexpected call from her ancestors, Moana must embark on a journey across the distant oceans of Oceania and venture into long-lost, treacherous waters for an adventure unlike any she has faced before.

Directed By

David Derrick Jr., Jason Hand, Dana Ledoux Miller

Produced By

Christina Chen, Yvett Merino

Cast

Auli'i Cravalho, Dwayne Johnson

MOANA 2 - PHOTOS

LITTLE SIS – In Walt Disney Animation Studios’ all-new feature film “Moana 2,” Moana embarks on an expansive new voyage to the far seas of Oceania—but now that she has a little sister, Simea, the stakes are so much higher. Simea looks up to Moana and misses her terribly when she’s gone. Leaving that adorable face is hard for Moana—even if the future of their people depends on it.

AN EXPANSIVE NEW VOYAGE – Walt Disney Animation Studios’ epic animated musical “Moana 2” sends Moana (voice of Auli‘i Cravalho) on an expansive new voyage alongside a crew of unlikely seafarers. Kele (voice of David Fane) is a farmer who knows which plants and produce can successfully survive the seas, Moni (voice of Hualālai Chung) is the designated storyteller, inspired by generations of oral storytelling in the Pacific Islands, and Loto (voice of Rose Matafeo) is a brilliant engineer in charge of Moana’s canoe.

A NEW MISSION – In Walt Disney Animation Studios’ all-new feature film “Moana 2,” Moana (voice of Auli‘i Cravalho) must journey to the far seas of Oceania and into dangerous, long-lost waters for an adventure unlike anything she’s ever faced. The ocean comes with impossible challenges: stormy seas, complicated curses and the Kakamora.
